About Us

Since its establishment, Coldspring Farmers Market Plus has been a community hub where local farmers, artisans, and customers come together and support one another. The home office is open Tuesday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Vendors gather on the courthouse lawn in Town Square on the 1st and 3rd Saturday of each month.

We are dedicated to providing our community with the freshest produce, meats, and artisan goods. Our vendors are local farmers and creators who take pride in their products, ensuring that you receive the best quality available. Feel free to shop all of our vendors online and connect with them directly so your products are fresh and ready for you to pick up at the market.
